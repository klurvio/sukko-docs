## FAQ

### Can I try Pro features before purchasing?

Yes. Contact us for a trial license key — full Pro features for 14 days.

### What happens when my license expires?

Sukko falls back to Community edition limits. Existing connections are not dropped, but new connections beyond Community limits are rejected.

### Is there a self-hosted requirement?

Yes. Sukko is BYOC (Bring Your Own Cloud). All editions run in your infrastructure. There is no hosted/SaaS option — you maintain full control of your data.
